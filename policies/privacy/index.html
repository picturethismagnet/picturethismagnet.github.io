Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

When you visit our site or place an order, we collect certain information to fulfill your order and improve your experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address when you place an order or contact us.
  • Photos: Images you upload for custom magnet production. These are used solely to create your ordered product.
  • Payment Information: Payment details are processed securely through Shopify and are never stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous browsing data such as pages visited, time on site, and referral source to improve our website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill and ship your orders
  • To communicate with you about your order, inquiries, or events
  • To improve our products and website experience
  • To send promotional emails (only with your consent; you can unsubscribe anytime)

Photo Privacy

We take your photo privacy seriously. Photos uploaded for magnet production are:

  • Used only to create your ordered product
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Not used for marketing without your explicit permission
  • Deleted from our production systems after order fulfillment

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Shopify: E-commerce platform for order processing and payments
  • FormSubmit: Contact form submissions
  • Google Fonts: Typography (no personal data collected)

Cookies

We use minimal cookies to maintain your shopping cart session and remember basic preferences. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@picturethismagnet.com.
