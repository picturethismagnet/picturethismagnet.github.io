Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Lily

PictureThisMagnet started with a simple idea: your favorite photos shouldn't just live on your phone. They deserve to be seen every day — on your fridge, your locker, your office board.

What began as a small passion project quickly grew into something bigger. Every custom magnet I create is a chance to help someone hold onto a memory, share a moment, or give a truly personal gift.

"Every photo tells a story. I just give it a magnetic home so you can relive it every single day."

I believe in quality craftsmanship, personal service, and the power of a simple photo to bring joy. Whether you're ordering for yourself, for a loved one, or for a special event, I put care into every single magnet.

Our Values:
  • Quality First — Every magnet is made with premium materials
  • Personal Touch — Your photos, your story, your magnets
  • Community — We love connecting with customers and their stories
