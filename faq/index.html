Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom photo magnets.

Our standard photo magnets are 2×2 inches — the perfect size for fridges, lockers, filing cabinets, and any magnetic surface. They're compact enough to collect and large enough to show off your favorite photos.

Visit our Shop page, choose your product (single magnets, bulk sets, or gift cards), upload your photo during checkout, and complete your purchase. It's that simple! If you need help, reach out through our Contact page.

Standard orders are processed and shipped within 3–5 business days. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Need it faster? Contact us about rush options.

We accept JPEG, PNG, and HEIC file formats. For the best print quality, use a high-resolution image (at least 300 DPI). Square or near-square images work best since our magnets are 2×2 inches. We'll crop your image to fit if needed.

Our magnets are printed on premium, UV-resistant material with a protective coating that resists scratches and fading. The magnetic backing is strong enough to hold on any standard fridge or magnetic surface.
